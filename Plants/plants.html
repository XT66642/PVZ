Plants Introduction

sunflower

COST: 50

Description: Sunflower is the second plant and first sun-producing plant received in the first Plants vs. Zombies game. She is a plant capable of generating sun over time, allowing the player to plant additional plants with ease.

Wallnut

COST: 50

Description: Wall-nut is the first defensive plant obtained Plants vs. Zombies and is the fourth plant unlocked overall. He is mainly used to stall or block zombies in order to buy time for the player to plant an offensive plant, or for offensive plants to take out zombies with high health.


PeaShooter

COST: 100

Description:Peashooter is the first and primary attacking plant received in the first Plants vs. Zombies game. He is a peashooting plant that shoots one pea every 1.5 seconds, dealing 20 damage.His peas can be lit on fire by Torchwood to deal twice as much damage.

Blover

COST: 100

Description: Blover is a single-use plant in Plants vs. Zombies as well as their Chinese spin-offs. He can be used to blow away fog on screen, along with any Balloon Zombies, instantly killing them in the process. This makes him an environment modifier.

SnowPea

COST: 175

Description:Snow Pea is a peashooting plant appearing in Plants vs. Zombies. He is unlocked after beating Level 1-6. Snow Pea attacks by firing frozen peas at zombies, which can slow their target's movement and attack speed by 50% and deal damage.



Splitpea

COST: 125

Description: Split Pea is a peashooting plant that is the combination of a forward-facing Peashooter and a backward-facing Repeater (without the dark green leaves), similar to conjoined twins. Obtained after beating the backyard Fog level 4-5.


Torchwood

COST: 110

Description:Torchwood is a recurring character within the Plants vs. Zombies franchise. While not being a pea himself, Torchwood's main role is boosting the power of peashooting plants by lighting them on fire, increasing their damage output.

TwinSunflower

COST: 150

Description:To get Twin Sunflower, the player must buy their seed packet from Crazy Dave's Twiddydinkies for $5000. They appear on sale after beating Level 3-4, along with the Gatling Pea. They cost 150 sun to plant and must be placed on an existing Sunflower.

Cornpult

COST: 100

Description:Kernel-pult is an offensive lobber plant in Plants vs. Zombies. While he normally lobs kernels that deal 20 damage at an equal rate to Cabbage-pult, he has a 25% chance to fire a butter projectile that deals 40 damage to his target.
